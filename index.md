# Privacy Policy for LetCheck

**Last updated: April 2026**
**Developer:** Massive Mouse
**Contact:** support@massivemouse.uk

---

## 1. Overview

LetCheck ("the app") is developed by Massive Mouse. This privacy 
policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information 
when you use LetCheck on your mobile device.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Your email address
- Your display name
- Authentication data via Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address 
from your Google account.

### Information You Provide
When using the app, you may enter:
- Property addresses
- Tenant and agent names
- Room and item descriptions
- Condition notes and annotations
- Agency name and contact details

### Photos
The app uses your device camera to capture property inspection 
photos. These photos are stored locally on your device only and 
are never uploaded to our servers.

### Usage Data
We collect limited usage data including:
- Number of PDF reports generated (stored in Firebase Firestore)
- Subscription and purchase status (managed by RevenueCat)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:
- Provide and maintain the LetCheck service
- Manage your account and subscription status
- Track your free PDF generation allowance
- Generate professional property inspection PDF reports
- Respond to support requests

---

## 4. Data Storage

### On-Device Storage
All property inspection data including reports, room data, photos, 
and annotations are stored locally on your device using encrypted 
local storage. This data is not uploaded to any server.

### Cloud Storage
The following limited data is stored in Firebase Firestore 
(Google LLC, servers in the European Union):
- Your account identifier
- Your PDF generation count
- Your subscription status

---

LetCheck uses the following third-party services:

**Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)**

Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Firebase Firestore (Google LLC)**

**RevenueCat Inc.**
Used to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases.
Privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

**Google Play Billing (Google LLC)**

Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal 
information to third parties. Data is only shared with the 
third-party services listed above as necessary to operate 
the app.

---

## 7. Data Retention

- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- PDF generation counts are deleted when your account is deleted
- On-device inspection data is deleted when you uninstall the app
- You may request deletion of your account and associated data 
  at any time by contacting support@massivemouse.uk

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

LetCheck is intended for use by property professionals and is 
not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not 
knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK resident you have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at 
support@massivemouse.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures 
to protect your personal information. Account data is protected 
by Firebase Authentication. All data transmission uses 
industry-standard TLS encryption.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will 
notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the 
top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes 
constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
