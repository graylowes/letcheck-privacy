# LetCheck — Account Deletion Request

To request deletion of your LetCheck account and all associated 
data, please email us at:

**support@massivemouse.uk**

Please include:
- The email address associated with your account
- Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion 
by email.

**What gets deleted:**
- Your account credentials
- Your PDF generation count
- All account data stored in our servers

**What is not affected:**
- Inspection data, photos and reports stored locally on your 
  device — these are never uploaded to our servers and can be 
  removed by uninstalling the app
